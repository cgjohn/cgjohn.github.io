Hey!

I'm Connor. I am currently finishing my junior at the University of Michigan studying computer science. When I am not doing school work I spend most of my time either playing tennis or working on improving the organizations I am a part of on campus.

MHacks:


MHacks is one of the world's premier collegiate hackathons. The event is hosted for 36 hours by students at the University of Michigan. My role in the organization is Director of Logistics for the 2,000 person event. This role includes transportation, food, and budgeting for the event. I have been involved for the past four events now and directed two of them.

Blueprints for Pangaea:


Blueprints For Pangaea (B4P) is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit medical surplus recovery organization (MSRO) that provides sustainable solutions to inefficient health care resource distribution. We work with hospitals around the nation to collect their unused medical supplies and ship them to third world countries in need. My role as CTO within the organization puts me in charge of the design and creation of our current website.

iXperience:


IXperience is a month long coding bootcamp in the beautiful city of Cape Town, South Africa. Through the program I learned HTML, CSS, and Angular JS. At the end of the bootcamp I was matched with a local company in which I served as an intern and was able to expand my knowledge of Angular in a work environment.
